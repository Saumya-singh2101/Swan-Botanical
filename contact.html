Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Swan Botanicals products unique?

Our products are crafted with 100% pure, ethically sourced botanical ingredients, handcrafted in small batches to ensure maximum potency and freshness. We are also committed to being cruelty-free and vegan.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, we formulate our products with sensitive skin in mind, avoiding harsh chemicals, fragrances, and irritants. However, we always recommend a patch test before full application.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within India. We are working to expand our shipping to international locations soon. Please check our shipping policy for the latest updates.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unopened and unused products. Please refer to our detailed return and refund policy page for more information.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also log into your account on our website to view order status.
